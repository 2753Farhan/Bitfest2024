Sweet Tomato Soup:
Ingredients:
- 2 kg Tomato
- 3 kg Alu
Instructions:
Blend tomatoes, add ALU, and cook for 20 minutes.

Caesar Salad:
Ingredients:
- 1 head Romaine Lettuce
- 1/2 cup Croutons
- 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
- Caesar Dressing
Instructions:
Toss lettuce with croutons, cheese, and dressing. Serve immediately.

Vegetable Stir-Fry
Ingredients:
* 2 cups Mixed Vegetables (carrots, broccoli, snap peas)
* 1cup Mushrooms, sliced
* 3cloves Garlic, minced
* 2tbsp Soy Sauce
* 1tbsp Sesame Oil
Instructions:
Heat sesame oil in a wok, add garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add vegetables and
mushrooms, cook for 5-7 minutes. Season with soy sauce and serve hot.
